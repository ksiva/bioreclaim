Our Story

Our products and solutions comprise of curated, select strains of probiotics and enzymes with microbial and enzymatic systems which function as a single unit to completely breakdown pollutants.  

We cater to various environmental issues by customizing consortiums of microbial strains in our state-of-the-art R&D facilities in order to achieve high efficiency of the breakdown of targeted pollutants.

Our proprietary microbial products are engineered to target and degrade a wide range of contaminants, effectively restoring soil health and vitality. Our products offer powerful, targeted enzymatic activity, accelerating the degradation of organic pollutants and facilitating soil remediation.
