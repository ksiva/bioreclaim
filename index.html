BioReclaim is a biotech company that rapidly cleans soil, air and water.

Nature,
restored
by nature.

We promote and protect biodiversity while renewing natural landscapes by identifying enzymes found in nature, like fungi, tropical trees and bacteria. 

When used, these enzymes remove industrial chemicals from the environment such as petroleum, pesticides, plastics and other man-made materials. 

Discovery, resilience, and collaboration are at the heart of everything we do—to benefit humans and nature alike.

Our industry calls it bio-remediation. We call it giving nature a fair chance at survival.

We integrate molecular biology, enzyme design and optimization, ecology and natural product development to help nature fight on it’s own. 

In 6-12 months, we can go from concept to bespoke solution for field testing. Want to learn more? Drop us a note.

Even small biomes need big data.
